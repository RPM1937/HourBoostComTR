🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 — HourBoost.com.tr

1. General Information

At HourBoost.com.tr, we value the privacy of our users greatly. This policy explains what data is collected when you use our site, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Your Steam passwords are not stored on our servers. They are only held in memory during the session and deleted after a secure connection to Steam is established.

2. Data Collected

The following data is collected when using our service:

  • Account information: Username and email address
  • Password: Your passwords are stored hashed using the PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm and are never stored in plain text
  • Steam username: The username of the account to be boosted
  • Boost history: Boost start/end times and number of games
  • Payment information: Plan type and payment amount (card details are processed by Shopier and are not transmitted to us)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser information (for security and rate limiting purposes)

3. Use of Data

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the service
  • Protecting account security
  • Plan and payment management
  • Resolving technical issues
  • Service development and improvement

Your data is not shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Our site uses the following cookies:

  • Session cookie: Required to maintain your login status. If rejected, you cannot log in.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Used to measure site usage. Can be rejected.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to show ads based on your interests. Can be rejected.

You can manage your cookie preferences from your browser settings or from our site's cookie preference panel.

5. Data Security

The following measures are taken to ensure the security of your data:

  • All connections are encrypted with HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
  • Passwords are stored with a strong hashing algorithm
  • Steam passwords are not stored on the server
  • Login attempts are protected with rate limiting

6. Data Retention Period

Data associated with your account is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be removed from the system within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • To learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • To request information about the data being processed
  • To request the correction or deletion of your data
  • To object to data processing

To exercise these rights, you can write to yuksekrpm@gmail.com.

8. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are used on our site:

  • Shopier: For payment processing. Your card details are processed only within Shopier's infrastructure.
  • Google AdSense: For ad display. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Steam (Valve): The Steam API is used for the game boost service.
  • Cloudflare: For security and CDN services.

9. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy: yuksekrpm@gmail.com
