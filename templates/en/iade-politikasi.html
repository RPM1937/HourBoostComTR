1. General Rule

❌ HourBoost.com.tr provides digital services. No refunds are issued after payment is completed and the service has been activated.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Changing your mind about using the plan after purchase

3. Exception Cases

Requests will be considered in the following situations:

  • Technical error: If the plan was not activated despite the payment appearing successful

In these cases, simply write to yuksekrpm@gmail.com with your proof of payment. Requests are responded to within 3 business days.

4. Subscription Cancellation

Subscriptions do not auto-renew. They remain in your account after a one-time payment. If you wish to delete your account, you can write to yuksekrpm@gmail.com.

5. Contact

For refund and cancellation requests: yuksekrpm@gmail.com

Please include your username and payment date in your email.
